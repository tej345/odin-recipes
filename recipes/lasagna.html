Veg Lasagna

Layers of tender pasta, hearty veggies, rich tomato sauce, and creamy cheese, baked to perfection—comforting, flavorful, and utterly satisfying!

Ingredients

  • 6 Lasagna Sheets (pre-cooked)
  • 1 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped Bell Peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped Zucchini
  • 1/2 cup Spinach
  • 1/2 cup Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 cup Mozarella
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasonings
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper (To Taste)

Recipe

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Veggie Filling
    • Heat olive oil in a pan.
    • Add bell peppers, zucchini, and spinach. Sauté for a few minutes with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  2. Layer the Lasagna
    • Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce.
    • Place a layer of pre-cooked lasagna sheets.
    • Add some sautéed veggies, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and parmesan on top.
  3. Bake & Serve
    • Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes until golden.
    • Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving!
