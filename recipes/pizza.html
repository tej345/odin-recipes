Cheese Pizza

Golden, crispy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and a hint of herbs—simple, cheesy, and irresistibly delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp Yeast
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce
  • 3/4 cup Warm Water
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella Cheese (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder

Recipe

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough
    • Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water; let it sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
    • Add flour, salt, and olive oil, then knead into a soft dough.
    • Cover and let it rise for an hour until doubled in size.
  2. Assemble the Pizza
    • Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • Roll out the dough into a thin round shape.
    • Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously.
    • Add oregano, chili flakes, and garlic powder for extra flavor.
  3. Bake & Serve
    • Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese melts and the crust turns golden brown.
    • Slice and serve hot!
