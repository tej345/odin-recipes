Veg Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 Burger Buns
  • 2 Potatoes (Boiled and Mashed)
  • 1/2 cup grated Carrots
  • 1/4 cup Peas (Boiled)
  • Few Green Chillies (Finely Chopped) (Optional)
  • 1 tsp Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 cup Breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour (For Binding)
  • Salt & Pepper (To Taste)
  • Lettuce, Tomato, Onion (sliced)
  • Mayonnaise

Recipe

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Patty
    • Mash boiled potatoes in a bowl.
    • Add grated carrot, boiled peas, garlic paste, green chili, red chili powder, garam masala, salt, and pepper.
    • Mix well, then add breadcrumbs and cornflour for binding. Shape into patties.
  2. Cook the Patty
    • Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the patties on medium heat until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  3. Assemble the Burger
    • Toast the burger buns lightly.
    • Spread mayonnaise or your preferred sauce on the bun.
    • Add lettuce, tomato, and onion slices.
    • Place the crispy patty on top and cover with the bun.
  4. Serve & Enjoy
    • Secure with a toothpick if needed, and serve hot with fries or a dip of choice!
